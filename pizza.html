PIZZA RECIPE

There are following step to prepare the Pizza

    Ingredients:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour, 1 packet instant yeast, 1 tsp sugar, 1 tsp salt, ¾ cup warm water, 2 tbsp olive oil, 1 cup pizza sauce, 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, veggies, etc.),

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Dough:
    • Combine flour, yeast, sugar, and salt in a bowl. Add warm water and olive oil, then knead until the dough is smooth. Let it rest for 30 minutes to rise.
  2. Preheat the Oven:
    • Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) and prepare a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  3. Roll Out the Dough:
    • Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness. Place it on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  4. Add Toppings:
    • Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, sprinkle mozzarella cheese, and add your favorite toppings.
  5. Bake the Pizza:
    • Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted. Remove, slice, and serve hot!
