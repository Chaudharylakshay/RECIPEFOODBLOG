SAMOSA RECIPE

There are following step to prepare the samosa

    Ingredients:

  • For the dough: All-purpose flour, ghee/oil, carom seeds, cumin seeds, salt, water
  • For the filling: Boiled potatoes, boiled green peas, onions, green chilies, ginger-garlic paste, spices (coriander powder, garam masala, turmeric, red chili powder), cilantro, lemon juice
  • For frying: Oil for deep frying
  1. Prepare the Dough
    • Mix 2 cups flour, 1/4 cup ghee, carom seeds, cumin seeds, and salt. Gradually add water to knead into a smooth, firm dough. Cover and let it rest for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Cook the Filling
    • Saut√© chopped onions, ginger-garlic paste, and green chilies in oil. Add boiled potatoes, peas, and spices (coriander, turmeric, red chili, garam masala), and cook until well mixed. Add salt, lemon juice, and chopped cilantro. Let it cool.
