Rasgulla Recipe

These are the following steps to prepare the Rasgulla

Ingredients:

  • 1 liter full-fat milk, 2-3 tablespoons lemon juice or vinegar (to curdle the milk), 1 cup sugar, 4 cups water, 2-3 cardamom pods (optional, for flavor)

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Chhena (Cottage Cheese):
    • Boil the milk in a heavy-bottomed pan. Once it begins to boil, lower the heat and gradually add lemon juice or vinegar while stirring continuously.
    • When the milk curdles completely and the whey separates, strain it through a muslin cloth.
    • Rinse the chhena under cold water to remove the sourness and squeeze out excess water gently.
  2. Knead the Chhena:
    • Place the chhena on a flat surface or plate and knead it for about 10 minutes until it is smooth and soft.
    • Divide the dough into small portions and roll them into smooth, crack-free balls (about the size of a walnut).
  3. Prepare the Sugar Syrup:
    • In a large pan, dissolve the sugar in water and bring it to a boil. Add cardamom pods if desired for flavor.
    • Make sure the pan is large enough to give the rasgullas space to expand while cooking.
  4. Cook the Rasgullas:
    • Drop the chhena balls gently into the boiling sugar syrup. Cover the pan and cook on medium heat for 15-20 minutes.
    • The rasgullas will puff up and double in size as they cook. Ensure the syrup remains boiling throughout the process.
  5. Cool and Serve:
    • Turn off the heat and allow the rasgullas to cool in the syrup. Once cooled, refrigerate them for a couple of hours.
    • Serve chilled, along with the syrup, for the best taste!
