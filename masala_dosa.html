Masala Dosa Recipe

These are the following steps to prepare the masala Dosa

Ingredients:

  • For the Dosa Batter: 1 cup rice, 1/4 cup urad dal (split black gram), 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds (methi), water, salt to taste.
  • For the Masala Filling: 2 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed, 1 onion, finely chopped, 1-2 green chilies, chopped, 1 tsp mustard seeds, 1/2 tsp turmeric powder, 1 tsp cumin seeds, curry leaves, salt to taste, 1-2 tbsp oil.

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Dosa Batter: Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds in water for about 4-6 hours. Drain and blend into a smooth batter. Let it ferment for 8-12 hours.
  2. Prepare the Masala Filling: Heat oil, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and curry leaves. Add onions and green chilies, saut√© until soft. Add turmeric powder, salt, and mashed potatoes. Cook for a few minutes.
  3. Prepare the Dosa: Heat a non-stick tawa. Grease with oil, pour dosa batter and spread it in a circular motion. Cook until golden brown on the edges.
  4. Fill the Dosa: Add the prepared masala filling in the center, fold both sides of the dosa to form a roll or half-moon shape.
  5. Serve: Serve the masala dosa hot with coconut chutney and sambar on the side.
